    • WhiskChat Server

    An advanced multimedia chatroom, based off CoinChat.

    Project maintained by WhiskTech Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Join/leave notifications

    Get notified when your friends sign on and log out. Don't be in the dark!

    Embedded Multimedia

    No need to paste links to your media. You can embed it right in-chat, and listen to it too! Great for sharing music with friends.

    Tipping

    Tip mWC (virtual money) to your friends. In WhiskChat, we made tipping easier to implement, for tipbots and clients alike.

    Earning

    Earn any amount from 0 to 1 mWC in one chat!

    Full BBCode support

    We fully support BBCode! A list of supported tags, with the code, can be found here.

    Desktop notifications

    If your browser supports WebKit notifications, you can get notifications when someone mentions you while you are doing other things! Never miss an important message again!

    Completely open source

    WhiskChat Server and Client are completely open source, both licenced under the GPL v2. Feel free to fork both client and server!
